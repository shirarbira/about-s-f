About me: I believe in the potential and promise of technology. For the past two and a half years I've been working in technology and development from a creative, editorial, and community-based role. I am seeking to facilitate and participate in these conversations and actions, particularly through strategizing, writing, creating, designing, programming and collaborating with others.
